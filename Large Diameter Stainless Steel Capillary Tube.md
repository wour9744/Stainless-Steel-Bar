## Material and Specifications

Our Large Diameter Stainless Steel Capillary Tubes are made from austenitic stainless steel grades such as AISI 304, AISI 316, and AISI 316L. These materials offer excellent resistance to oxidation, pitting, and chloride stress corrosion cracking. The tubes are also available in other grades like AISI 430 for specialized applications.
